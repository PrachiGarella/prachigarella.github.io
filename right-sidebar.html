Social Media

My life as a social media content creator

Youtube

My youtube journey started in collaboration with my boyfriend when we went to England for our masters. My boyfriend (also a physicist) shares a passion for travel (along with passion for physics) and as a consequence started a channel called the 'The Knowmads' where we started posting our travel vlogs in early 2019. However due to Covid-19 travel came to a hault and so did the vlogs. In 2020 I started my own YouTube Channel where I shared by literature reviews, daily vlogs, my love for meditation and a lot more. Podcast

After a short yet successful run of our travel vlogs, where we explored our nomadic side, recently my boyfriend and I have started a podcast called 'The Knowmads Podcast' to cater to the 'Know' of Knowmads. This podcast is about Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Music, Relationships, Philosophy, Culture, Graduate life and much more. This podcast is available on any major podcast streaming platform including Apple Podcast and Spotify. We are really excited to invite different people and if you would like to be on the podcast just write to us at theknowmadsofficial@gmail.com

Instagram

I started posting on instagram in early 2018. Initially I posted about travel and fashion but later I started to include my journey through academia. I feel content creation has drastically changed the face of social media in the past five years. My number one rule as a content creator is that I only make content that is true to myself and resonates my beliefs. I am strictly against promoting things that I personally don't accept. Since this is not my full time profession, I consider it as a digital memory storing platform for my future self that also serves as a medium to share my thoughts, journey and knowledge.
