Social Media

My life as a social media content creator

Youtube

My youtube journey started in collaboration with my boyfriend when we went to England for our masters. My boyfriend (also a physicist) shares a passion for travel (along with passion for physics) and as a consequence started a channel called the 'The Knowmads' where we started posting our travel vlogs in early 2019. However due to Covid-19 travel came to a hault and so did the vlogs. In 2020 I started my own YouTube Channel where I shared by literature reviews, daily vlogs, my love for meditation and a lot more. Instagram

I started posting on instagram in early 2018. Initially I posted about travel and fashion but later I started to include my journey through academia. I feel content creation has drastically changed the face of social media in the past five years. My number one rule as a content creator is that I only make content that is true to myself and resonates my beliefs. I am strictly against promoting things that I personally don't accept. Since this is not my full time profession, I consider it as a digital memory storing platform for my future self that also serves as a medium to share my thoughts, journey and knowledge.
